# Jaqueline Leite Vieira

**Researcher · Tissue engineering, biomaterials and 3D cell culture**

Jaqueline is a researcher at the bioengineering laboratory, working in a biosafety level 2 (NB2) environment. Her work involves the three-dimensional culture of human and animal cells in bioreactors, focused on processes that require continuous and rigorous monitoring of environmental conditions.

Interested in the intersection of science and technology, Jaqueline adopts IoT solutions to instrument and supervise critical laboratory equipment, transforming analog processes into real-time, remotely monitorable data streams.
