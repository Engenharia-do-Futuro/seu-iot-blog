# Rodrigo Schardong

**Creator of Seu IoT · Computer Engineer**

Rodrigo holds a degree in Computer Engineering from UERGS and has been passionate about IoT technology since his undergraduate years. He created Seu IoT as his first venture — originally to accelerate his own connected projects — and quickly realized the platform could solve the same problem for many other developers.

What started as a personal tool grew into a full-featured platform used to build IoT solutions with quality, security, and speed. Today, Rodrigo focuses on refining the platform and expanding the Seu IoT ecosystem, with the goal of making connected product development more accessible for teams and entrepreneurs of all sizes.
